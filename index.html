What is this?

As a freelancer, I rely on 3rd party payment service providers, like Toptal Payments, and Payoneer to access my earning. Such services usually have a fee for each transaction.

Everytime I go to withdraw money from the debit card provided by these services, I have to first pull out a calculator and figure out how much I can withdraw, given the amount of money in my card. This utility web app is built to replace that friction point.
